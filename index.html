Favorite Recipes

Homemade Pizza

60 min

A simple yet delicious homemade pizza recipe with a crispy crust and your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 tsp yeast
  • Your favorite toppings
Instructions:
  1. Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, knead into dough
  2. Let rise for 30 minutes in a warm place
  3. Roll out, add toppings, bake at 475°F for 12-15 minutes

Engineer's Coffee

10 min

My personal recipe for the perfect cup of coffee to fuel late-night project sessions.

Ingredients:
  • 30g freshly ground coffee beans
  • 500ml filtered water
  • 1 tbsp maple syrup (optional)
  • Dash of cinnamon
Instructions:
  1. Heat water to 205°F
  2. Pour over ground coffee in a slow circular motion
  3. Let brew for 4 minutes, stir, then press
  4. Add maple syrup and cinnamon if desired
